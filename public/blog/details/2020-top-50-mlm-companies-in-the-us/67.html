2020 Top 50 MLM Companies in the U.S.
  • 01 December, 2020

*Note: 2019 revenue numbers coming soon.


Below is the comprehensive list of multi-level marketing companies generating the most amount of revenue in the United States. After compiling this list, we noticed some interesting trends:


Only 2 companies in the Top 10 were founded after the year 2000. Out of the Top 50, only 20 were founded after 2000.

38% (19 total) of the Top 50 MLMs were in the supplements industry, by far the most popular category. The Beauty industry is second with 16% (8 total).

Of the Top 10, 50% of the companies are in the Beauty industry.
